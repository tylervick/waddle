# BoomBox Privacy Policy

BoomBox does not collect any data of any kind.

- The app makes no network connections.
- There are no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that phone
  home.
- WAD files you import stay on your device. They are never uploaded or
  shared anywhere.

The App Store itself may collect standard install and crash metrics; those
are governed by [Apple's privacy policies](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/),
not ours.

Questions? Open an issue on this repository.
